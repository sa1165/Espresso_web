The Roasting Science

Precise Roasting & Temperature Control

Our specialty roasting profiling records temperature profiles at 1-second intervals. We target the first crack at exactly 196°C to unlock delicate floral aromas without charred bitterness.

When brewing, we utilize reverse-osmosis filtered water heated to a exact 93.5°C to extract coffee solids with scientific precision, matching our target TDS of 1.35%.
